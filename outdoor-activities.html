Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring my own snorkeling gear?

While we provide snorkeling gear as part of the tour package, you're welcome to bring your own if you prefer.

How long are the standard tours?

The duration of the standard tours varies depending on the package you choose. Full-day tours typically last around 6-8 hours, while half-day tours last approximately 3-4 hours.

What happens if the weather is bad on the day of my tour?

In the event of bad weather, we may need to reschedule or cancel your tour. Rest assured, we'll keep you informed every step of the way and work with you to find a suitable solution.

Is there a refund policy if I need to cancel my booking?

Our cancellation policy allows for flexible cancellations with a full refund if you need to cancel your booking for any reason. Please refer to our terms and conditions for more information.

Are there any discounts available for group bookings?

Yes, we offer group discounts for parties of ten or more. Contact us for more information on group booking rates and special perks.
